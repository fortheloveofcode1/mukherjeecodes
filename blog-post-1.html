Check-In:

Biometric authentication systems at check-in counters or self-service kiosks use facial recognition algorithms to capture and analyze passengers' facial features. These systems compare the captured biometric data against stored templates from travel documents or databases to verify passengers' identities. By automating the identity verification process, biometric check-in systems reduce reliance on manual document checks, thereby improving operational efficiency and minimizing queues.

Security Screening:

Biometric authentication technologies are deployed at security checkpoints to enhance identity verification and security screening procedures. Facial recognition systems analyze passengers' facial characteristics in real-time, matching them against pre-registered biometric templates or watchlists. Similarly, fingerprint scanning devices capture and analyze passengers' fingerprint patterns for identification purposes. By integrating biometric authentication into security screening processes, airports bolster security measures while expediting passenger throughput.

Boarding Gates:

Biometric boarding gates leverage facial recognition technology to authenticate passengers prior to boarding aircraft. As passengers approach the boarding gate, facial recognition cameras capture their facial images and compare them against stored biometric templates. Upon successful verification, the boarding gate grants access, allowing passengers to board without presenting physical boarding passes or identification documents. This streamlined boarding process reduces boarding times and enhances operational efficiency for airlines.

How to Register on the DigiYatra Mobile App

  • Download the App: Install the DigiYatra app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  • Start Registration: Tap "Get Started" and enter your mobile phone number.
  • Verify Phone Number: Enter the OTP received via SMS to verify your number.
  • Access Wallet Feature: Navigate to the "Wallet" option at the bottom of the screen.
  • Add Identity Credentials: Select "Identity credential" from the drop-down menu, agree to the terms, and upload your identity documents.
  • Complete Registration: Once your documents are uploaded, your DigiYatra ID will be generated using Aadhaar-based validation and a self-captured image.

Airport Process Using DigiYatra

  • E-Gate Entry: Scan your boarding pass or e-ticket at the E-Gate. This can be a printout or a soft copy on your mobile device.
  • Validation: The system checks your passenger and flight details.
  • Identity Verification: Upon successful verification of your Ticket and DigiYatra ID, the E-Gate is activated.
  • Single Token Creation: Your face, combined with the ticket PNR, is used to create a single identity token for the journey.
  • Facial Recognition: Your identity is confirmed through facial recognition as you proceed.
  • Boarding: Access the security area and board the aircraft via the e-Gate using the facial recognition system.
